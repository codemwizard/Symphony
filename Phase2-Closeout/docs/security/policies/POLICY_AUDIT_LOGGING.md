# Audit Logging Retention & Review Policy (Phase-0 Stub, Tier-1 Ready)

## Purpose
This policy defines audit logging requirements to support:
- PCI DSS logging controls
- ISO 27001/27002 monitoring and review expectations
- regulator-ready incident reconstruction (BoZ / internal audit)

Phase-0 establishes **mandatory retention and review intent** and binds it to mechanical evidence gates.

## Scope
In scope:
- database audit logs (including OpenBao/Vault audit device logs)
- CI evidence artifacts generated by verification scripts
- access logs for administrative and privileged operations

Out of scope (Phase-0):
- centralized SIEM deployment and managed SOC operations (Phase-1/2)
- long-term WORM storage anchoring execution (schema hooks only in Phase-0)

## Retention
Minimum retention targets (to be tailored per customer contract and regulator expectations):
- **Hot retention**: 90 days readily searchable
- **Warm retention**: 12 months accessible for audit and investigations
- **Cold retention**: 7 years for financial control evidence (or longer if required by customer/regulator)

## Time Synchronization
- all systems producing audit logs must use a time source (NTP/chrony)
- timestamps must be in UTC in all evidence artifacts

## Review and Alerting
- privileged access and key events are reviewed at least daily in operational environments
- automated alerts are configured for anomalous patterns as the platform matures (Phase-1/2)

## Integrity and Tamper Evidence
- audit logs must be append-only in the collection system
- evidence artifacts emitted by CI must include provenance metadata (git SHA, inputs checked, schema fingerprint)
