# Phase 4 Capability Boundary

Status: Prepared, Not Open

## Authorized Capability Domains

Phase 4 owns the deterministic financial and statutory consequences of admitted
decisions:

- settlement finality boundaries
- BoZ FX reference-rate authority
- asset-to-settlement hard binding
- currency legality gates
- statutory deductions and allocations
- statutory kill criteria

## Prohibited Capability Domains

Phase 4 must not implement:

- any AI decision surface
- Phase 5 methodology runtime or adapters
- Phase 6 UI/operator workflows
- Phase 8 authorization, registry, or tokenization integrations
- informal/manual-only finality or statutory calculations

## Future-Phase Routing

- methodology execution routes to Phase 5
- operator workspaces route to Phase 6
- external authorization routes to Phase 8A
- registry bridges route to Phase 8B
- tokenization routes to Phase 8C

## Mandatory Closeout Provision

Phase 4 planning must preserve future-phase isolation by leaving Phase 5 in a
non-claimable stub posture before any Phase 4 completion claim.
