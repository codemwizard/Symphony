**Yes, Symphony does address AI, but not in the way most people expect.**

* AI enters only after the system has a strong legitimacy and provenance substrate.

Most green economy platforms make a critical mistake:

* probabilistic,
* non-deterministic,
* subject to hallucination,
* hard to replay exactly,
* difficult to audit.

Symphony’s constitutional design correctly treats AI as:

> an advisory computation layer operating on a deterministic evidence substrate.

That is exactly how a sovereign-grade system should be built.
