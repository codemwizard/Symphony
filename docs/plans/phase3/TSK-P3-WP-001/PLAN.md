## Proof Boundaries

- Guarantees:
  - The verifier can prove that typed dependency declarations exist and are
    machine-traversable where inspected.
  - The verifier can prove that incomplete dependency insertion fails under
    structural constraints.
- Limitations:
  - The verifier cannot prove semantic correctness of every dependency
    relationship.
  - The verifier cannot prove policy or authority lineage semantics.
  - The verifier cannot prove runtime API integration.
