How to present this in the demo
Backing mode: DEMO_BACKED for Phase 1. Symphony is not a fraud detection platform. The SIM-swap hold is one example of programme-configurable risk conditions — the same mechanism that holds for GPS mismatch or missing proofs. The MFI chose to include a 30-day MSISDN registration threshold in their policy. Symphony enforces it and records it. The story is: governed green disbursement can incorporate operational risk thresholds defined by the FSP, without Symphony becoming the arbiter of fraud. Spend 60–90 seconds here maximum. The main story is evidence-backed release, not risk interception.
